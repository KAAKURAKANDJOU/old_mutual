What is Life Insurance with DigiLife Loan Protection?

This plan protects your loved ones financially if something happens to you. It also includes DigiLife Loan Protection – meaning if you have a loan (car, house, or personal), the insurance will pay off the remaining loan so your family is not left with debt.

Key Benefits

  • Life cover from P50,000 up to P1,000,000
  • DigiLife Loan Protection – your loan is paid off automatically
  • Monthly premiums that fit your budget
  • Covers spouse and children (optional add-ons)
  • Fast claims settlement within 48 hours
  • Tax-free payout to your family
