Practical Tips for Batswana Families

1. Start a Small Emergency Fund

Try to save at least 3 months of expenses. Even P100 per month adds up quickly and gives you peace of mind.

2. Protect What Matters Most

Life insurance and funeral cover are not expenses — they are investments in your family’s future.

3. Insure Your Car and Home

Accidents and theft can happen anytime. Allsure short-term insurance protects your biggest assets.

4. Teach Your Children About Money

Start early — involve your kids in family budgeting and saving goals. It builds strong financial habits.

5. Avoid Unnecessary Debt

Only borrow what you can comfortably repay. Use DigiLife Loan Protection so your family is never left with debt.

6. Save for Big Goals

Use our Family Savings & Investments plan for school fees, weddings, or your children’s future.
