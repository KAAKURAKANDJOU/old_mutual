What is Ka-Metlha Funeral Cover?

Ka-Metlha means “every day”. This is a simple and affordable funeral cover plan created especially for Batswana families. It helps you give your loved ones a dignified and respectful farewell without putting financial stress on the family.

Key Benefits

  • Covers the main member, spouse, and children
  • Flexible cover from P10,000 up to P100,000 per person
  • Monthly premiums starting from only P50
  • Fast claims paid within 48 hours
  • No medical examination required
  • Can be used for funeral expenses, transport, and memorial service
